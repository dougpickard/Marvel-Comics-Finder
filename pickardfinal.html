About Us

Welcome to Non Sequitor News. We love comics books and we're learning to love web development. With comic companies increasingly making their content avaiable online, we're expirementing with various APIs to display cover art. Our first example uses the Marvel Comics API (probably the most developed such API at this point). Type in any year in the filter and we'll return five random comic covers. You can see how the art and style has developed through the 20th century.Let us know what you think! Check out the Marvel API, too!
